# QuadraILearn Account Deletion

Last updated: March 20, 2026

## How to Request Deletion

To request deletion of a QuadraILearn account, contact:

- support@quadravise.com

Include:

- registered email address
- account role
- whether the request applies to mobile, web, or both

## What Happens After Request

- we verify account ownership
- we review any active dependencies linked to guardian or admin relationships
- we process deletion or anonymization as required by operational and legal constraints

## Typical Scope

Deletion may include:

- account profile information
- authentication records not required for compliance or fraud prevention
- uploaded profile image data
- linked app access

Certain records may be retained where required for security, compliance, billing, abuse prevention, or audit obligations.
