# QuadraILearn Product PRD

## Product Vision
QuadraILearn is a Learning Health Platform that measures how well a student is learning.

Instead of lectures or courses, the system focuses on:
- Learning diagnostics
- Learning health score
- Learning streaks
- Leaderboards
- Guardian visibility

## Core Value
Students know exactly:
- which concepts they are weak in
- how consistent they are
- how their learning health improves
